Yoga Fest Free Event

Dublin City Marlay Park


Starts from 25th Feb-17th March every sunday @ 11am

What is Yoga Fest?

The Yoga Fest promotes a sense of sharing and community-lifting each other up and cheering each other on. People of all ages are welcomed equally at Yoga Fest, you will find something for everyone, whether that is a kids yoga class, a breathwork circle, the craft market or through music and dance!Breathing and moving together creates a sense of belonging with others around you and really brings the uniting practice of yoga to the forefront. Taking yoga outdoors is a change to your usual routine. By practicing outside, you will appreciate the beauty in the little things you normally take for granted. Contact Us:00-234

Where is the Event?

Yoga In The Park will be taking place from Feb 25th every sunday for 4 weeks at 11am on the Dublin city Marlay Park. All you need is a mat, a bottle of water and comfortable clothing. The event will comprise of a 75 minute yoga class presented by one of the Yoga team in Dublin @Marlay Park. All levels of yogis or non-yogis and families are welcome or those who would like to discover or are curious of the many benefits from yoga.Being outdoors fully awakens your senses. There is nothing like a cool gust of wind, the smell of blooming flowers, the song of birds and the noise of the wind to make you present.
